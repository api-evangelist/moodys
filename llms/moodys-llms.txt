# Moody's

> Moody's provides APIs spanning KYC and compliance (kompany KYC API), economic data and forecasting (Data Buffet, Scenario Studio), credit risk analytics (EDF-X, Consumer Credit Loss, Municipal Probability of Default), automotive residual values (AutoCycle), insurance and catastrophe modeling (Risk Modeler, Intelligent Risk Platform, Location Intelligence, Climate on Demand), financial spreading (QUIQspread), commercial real estate, and news aggregation (NewsEdge). Access is enterprise-contracted; the economy.com APIs authenticate with OAuth2 client credentials.

Generated by API Evangelist from the moodys catalog repo (no provider-published /llms.txt found on www.moodys.com, www.economy.com, developer.rms.com, hub.moodysanalytics.com, or www.kompany.com — the 200 responses on moodys.com and economy.com are HTML app shells).

## APIs

- [Moody's KYC API](https://www.kompany.com/kycapi/discover): KYC, KYB, AML, UBO discovery and entity verification (kompany).
- [Moody's Data Buffet API](https://www.economy.com/products/tools/data-buffet): economic, demographic, and financial time series and forecasts. Base URL https://api.economy.com/data/v1
- [Moody's Scenario Studio API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): custom macroeconomic scenarios from the Scenario Studio platform. Base URL https://api.economy.com/scenario-studio/v2
- [Moody's AutoCycle API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): vehicle price and residual value forecasts. Base URL https://api.economy.com/autocycle/v1
- [Moody's Consumer Credit Loss Forecasts API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): expected consumer credit loss forecasts under baseline and stress scenarios.
- [Moody's Municipal Probability of Default API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): municipal probability of default and loss rates. Base URL https://api.economy.com/muni/v1
- [Moody's EDF-X API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): probability of default for ~400 million companies via the Orbis database.
- [Moody's NewsEdge API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): real-time news from 24,000+ sources.
- [Moody's QUIQSpread API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): automated financial statement spreading.
- [Moody's Capital Risk Analyzer API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): capital ratio and credit metric projections for stress testing.
- [Moody's Climate on Demand API](https://developer.rms.com/climate-on-demand): physical climate risk scoring.
- [Moody's Location Intelligence API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): 100+ hazard, risk score, and exposure data layers.
- [Moody's Risk Modeler API](https://developer.rms.com/risk-modeler): end-to-end catastrophe modeling workflows.
- [Moody's Intelligent Risk Platform API](https://developer.rms.com/platform/docs/introduction): platform REST APIs for Risk Modeler, UnderwriteIQ, TreatyIQ, and ExposureIQ tenants.
- [Moody's Commercial Real Estate API](https://hub.moodysanalytics.com/products): commercial location scores and CRE data.
